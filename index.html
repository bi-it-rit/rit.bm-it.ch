About Me

Hello! My name is Leandro and I'm 17 years old. I live in Niederhasli, a peaceful town in Zurich Switzerland.

On the technical side, I have some familiarity with HTML, CSS, and Java, which complements the practical knowledge I’m gaining in my apprenticeship. Coding is something that excites me, especially because my dream is to become a game developer. The idea of creating immersive worlds and experiences that others can enjoy is a driving force behind my passion for programming.

I enjoy spending my time diving into the world of video games and music. Both are essential escapes that fuel my creativity and relaxation. Whether it's playing the latest RPG or discovering new music, these hobbies allow me to unwind after a busy day.

Currently, I’m doing an apprenticeship at ESPAS, where I’ve gained a wealth of knowledge and valuable experience. This opportunity has allowed me to grow both personally and professionally, and I’m grateful for the skills I’m developing here.

In addition to these interests, I’m passionate about staying active and regularly work out at the gym. Fitness is important to me, and I love the feeling of pushing myself to achieve new goals.
